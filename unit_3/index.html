What is TUYU?

TUYU is an on-demand mobile beauty service that brings the polish of a professional nail salon to your home, office, hotel. Benefiting women on both ends of the nail polish pot, it presents a flexible working platform for the nail therapists and gives busy women the chance to book treatments that fit with their schedule and lifestyle.
It was an idea born from personal experience and a beauty solution for time poor women eager to find a better work/‘me time’ balance.
